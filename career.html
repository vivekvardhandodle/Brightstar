We are Hiring


ARE YOU EXPERIENCED IN DRIVING, CUSTOMER SERVICE OR PATIENT CARE? JOIN THE SUCCESS TEAM AT BrightStar Non-Medical Transportationation LLC

This position focuses on non-emergency medical transportation, but we welcome candidates with experience in customer service, home health-care, and providing high-quality patient or other care. BrightStar Non-Medical Transportationation proudly serves our communities with ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) transportation. Come join the largest private sector operator of public transportation in North America – one that cares about its employees, passengers and their communities.

WHY DRIVE WITH US?

  • Top paid
  • Good Sign-On Bonus for 6 months empl
  • Paid Training on the job
  • Starting salary $20 hour and up to $25 per hour with prior experience and passes the required process.
  • Fixed schedule/routine routes
  • Ability to advance your career

Apply today for immediate consideration!


RESPONSIBILITIES:

The ability to transport passengers throughout the Maryland, Virginia, Washington D.C. areas in a safe and courteous manner Greet passengers, ensure fare payment and maintain a safe environment. shifts assigned based on business need: Either 6 AM or 5 PM weekdays, and some weekends. Each employee will be required to work requested shifts, which includes weekdays, evenings, and weekend shifts.

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Must be at least 21 years old or older with a high school diploma, GED or equivalent
  • Must have a clean driving record in one of this MD, DC, or VA
  • Must be able to provide a 5-year certified driving record – this can be obtained by your local Motor Vehicle Authority, or your driving record can be printed at our facility for a small fee
  • Must possess excellent customer service and communication skills
  • Must be punctual with an emphasis on your attendance
  • Must submit to drug testing, an extensive criminal background check
  • Must be able to work in all types of weather
  • Must be able to complete driver training/pass certification
  • Physical Requirements Include Ability to:
    • Sit for extended periods of time
    • Lift, push, pull, and carry tools, objects, or equipment above shoulder level without assistance
    • Move up and down stairs easily
    • Reach overhead and below the knees, including bending, twisting, pulling, and stooping
